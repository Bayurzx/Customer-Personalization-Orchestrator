URGENT TONE INSTRUCTIONS:

Create a message with urgency and time-sensitivity. This tone should:
- Emphasize scarcity, limited-time offers, or immediate action needed
- Use action-oriented language and strong verbs
- Create a sense of FOMO (fear of missing out)
- Include time-bound language (e.g., "limited time", "expires soon", "act now")
- Maintain professionalism while conveying urgency
- Use shorter, punchier sentences
- Include urgent call-to-action phrases

URGENT TONE CHARACTERISTICS:
- Scarcity-focused: "Limited availability", "Only X left", "Exclusive access"
- Time-sensitive: "Expires in 24 hours", "Last chance", "Don't wait"
- Action-oriented: "Act now", "Secure your spot", "Claim today"
- Direct and concise language
- Strong emotional triggers related to missing out

SUBJECT LINE STYLE:
- Use urgent words: "Last Chance", "Expires Soon", "Limited Time"
- Include numbers or deadlines when relevant
- Keep it punchy and direct

BODY STYLE:
- Start with urgency hook
- Quickly establish the time constraint or scarcity
- Provide clear, immediate value proposition
- End with strong, urgent call-to-action
- Use exclamation points sparingly but effectively

AVOID:
- Being pushy or aggressive
- Creating false urgency
- Overwhelming the reader with too many urgent elements
