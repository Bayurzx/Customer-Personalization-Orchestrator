### Design Guidelines

#### **Visual Style**
- Use Azure blue color scheme (#0078D4, #004E89)
- Clean, professional design with plenty of white space
- Consistent font sizing and hierarchy
- High-quality icons and graphics

#### **Content Guidelines**
- Keep bullet points concise (max 7 words per bullet)
- Use data visualization for metrics and results
- Include compelling statistics and numbers
- Maintain consistent messaging throughout

#### **Technical Elements**
- Architecture diagrams showing agent flow
- Performance charts and metrics dashboards
- Code snippets where relevant (minimal)
- Screenshots of actual system outputs
